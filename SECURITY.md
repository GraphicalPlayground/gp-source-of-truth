Our Security Approach

The Information Security Team at **Graphical Playground** is continually working to protect our
users, data, products, and services.

Our team understands that there is an incredible community of talented security researchers who want
to help further strengthen our security.

If you think you've found a vulnerability on one of our sites or within our products, please report
it to us by either emailing <mailto:security@graphical-playground.com>, or by visiting
[graphical-playground.com/security](https://graphical-playground.com/security).
Once submitted, our team will work with you to validate and resolve your findings as appropriate.

When submitting your vulnerability report, please include as much detail about your finding as
possible, such as domain names, impacted services, steps to reproduce, and any other relevant
information. Please be sure to thoroughly review our program policy prior to submission.

We look forward to hearing from you!
